We’re the salon experience you’ve been begging for—inherently cool but inclusive, chic AF but so easy—and it doesn’t hurt that we’re obsessed with making you look and feel amazing.

One of the cool things about us is that we pride ourselves on being human-forward—that means we don’t just value the people that sit in our chairs, but those that stand behind them, as well.

We believe in redefining the salon experience in every way and pushing the limits on what's "possible." For our team that looks like limitless growth and being surrounded by like minded people and for our clients, well— 100% talent, 0% pretentious.

Thoughtfully reimagined—this is your The Coco Salon and Academy.

The beauty industry sends 877 pounds of waste to landfills every single minute. We're changing that.
‍
The Coco Salon and Academy is a certified green salon. Through our partnership with Green Circle we're able to recycle and repurpose up to 95% of the salons waste.

Whether composted, turned into clean energy, or broken down and repurposed, you can rest assured that when choosing The Coco Salon and Academy you're choosing sustainability.
