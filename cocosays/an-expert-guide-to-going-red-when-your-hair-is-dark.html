The Ultimate Guide to Rocking Red Hair for Brunettes

Posted on 
June 26, 2023
 in 
Hair Color
H

ello there, beautiful brunettes! Are you ready to set the world ablaze with your hair game? It's time to ignite your locks and dive into the captivating realm of red. Who says blondes have all the fun?

Brunettes can rock the red like no other, and we're here to guide you through the process of transforming your dark tresses into a fiery masterpiece—all while keeping your hair's health in check.

Now, get ready to embark on a journey that will leave you breathless as we explore the magical world of red hair. We’ve brought in Amél, master colorist at The Coco Salon and Academy, to guide us through.

For all our virgins out there: Embrace the Flame Without Bleach

You may think that going from brown to red is a hair-raising feat, but fear not! Unleashing your inner redhead is not as daunting as it seems. Your dark locks are a hidden treasure trove of natural red tones just waiting to be discovered. When we unveil those vibrant hues, your hair will radiate like a glorious sunset, revealing a stunning red hue without the need for bleach. The best part? This technique works wonders on virgin hair, so if you've never colored your locks before, consider yourself lucky!

Dive in and go for a big bold red, because you can. With virgin hair above all else, work with an expert in reds. You want to stear clear of hot roots and avoid bleach when unnecessary. Reds already the highest maintenance color so I like to avoid adding any unnecessary processes. Other than that, there’s a shade of red for everyone, If you go for an all over red be prepared to be in the salon every 4 weeks. -Amél

Transitioning From Processed Hair to Red

If you're a color connoisseur with a history of pastels or purples, transitioning to red might require some extra tender loving care. Remember, color doesn't lift color, so if you've already ventured into the world of hair color, your path to red may require a little more finesse. But fret not, for our expert colorists are here to work their magic. They possess a wealth of knowledge and skill to infuse your tresses with fiery red and copper tones, setting the stage for that magnificent new shade of red that will leave you breathless.

Whether you've had previous color treatments or a double process, a color correction might be in order. This process involves a seasoned colorist carefully analyzing your current hair color, identifying any unwanted undertones, and formulating a custom color blend to create a seamless transition to your desired shade of red. Here's what Amél has to say about the transition.

Not so virgin brunettes: 

The good thing about not being a virgin is that you lift warm and warmth is exactly what we need for red heads. You might not be able to delve into strawberry blondes or golden copper tones right away but you can definitely play in the world of copper and red violets. 

A different shade of red:

This is a fun spectrum to play with! You can bounce back and forth between Jessica Chastain and Zendaya in Euphoria by playing with shades and adding more gold or blue to your formula.

Discover Your Perfect Shade of Red

Before you dive headfirst into the vibrant world of red, it's crucial to find the red hue that sets your soul on fire. Your skin's undertones hold the secret to unlocking the perfect red recipe for you. It's time to embrace your inner chameleon and determine whether your undertones lean towards cool or warm tones.

If you rock cool undertones with hints of blue, green, or violet, get ready to dazzle with a red-brown auburn look that will leave everyone in awe. This sophisticated shade exudes confidence and radiates an air of unapologetic allure.

For those blessed with warm undertones, boasting shades of red, orange, gold, or yellow, allow yourself to be swept away by the captivating allure of copper or bellini tones. Imagine your tresses cascading like a breathtaking sunset, commanding attention with every step you take.

Don't forget to consider your eye color and natural hair texture, as different shades can work their magic in unique ways, accentuating your hair's individual charm and charisma. A consultation with one of our expert colorists will ensure you find the perfect red hue that plays well with your features and personal style.

The Red Routine: Nurture Your Fiery Mane

Red hair is undeniably a showstopper, but it requires a little extra tender loving care compared to other shades. To keep your fiery locks vibrant and healthy between salon visits, it's essential to pamper them with top-notch color-safe products that provide nourishment and protection. Elevate your hair care routine with the crème de Pune crème of color-preserving products, such as Shu Uemura's Color Lustre line. These exceptional formulations will help maintain that fresh-from-the-salon look, ensuring your red mane radiates with brilliance.

‍

To further enhance the longevity of your red hue, incorporate a leave-in conditioner into your daily routine. We can't get enough of Shiki Worker, a magical potion that seals the hair cuticle, locks in moisture, and imparts a heavenly shine. By lavishing your locks with these rejuvenating products, you'll witness your red hair flourishing like a fiery phoenix.

‍

Remember, the sun, saltwater, and heat styling are the arch-nemeses of vibrant red hair. Shield your locks from their harmful effects with proper protection. Invest in heat protectants, UV shields, and hair accessories that keep your red mane shielded from the elements. This way, your dazzling red will remain untarnished.

As for touch-ups, depending on your chosen red shade and your natural hair color, we recommend revisiting your favorite colorist every four to eight weeks. By minimizing new hair growth (no more than an inch), you'll maintain even color and avoid the need for extensive color corrective services down the line. Embrace this opportunity to indulge in a little self-care, for every visit to the salon is a chance to rejuvenate your fiery spirit.

So, magnificent brunette goddesses, are you ready to embrace your inner redheaded enchantress? The path to becoming a ravishing redhead may seem like a daring adventure, but fear not, for you hold the power to captivate the world with your fiery locks. With the right shade, proper care, and the guidance of our skilled The Coco Salon and Academy colorists, your transformation into a mesmerizing redhead will leave everyone spellbound.
